Hong, Ji Yeon, and Wenhui Yang. 2020. “Oilfields, Mosques and Violence: Is There a Resource Curse in Xinjiang?” _British Journal of Political Science_ 50(1): 45-78. <https://doi.org/10.1017/S0007123417000564>.
